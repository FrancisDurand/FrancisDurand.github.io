Francis Durand

I'm a first-year PhD student in mathematics and theoretical computer science, working on combinatorics. I’m particularly interested in random generation, symbolic methods, and the interface between combinatorics and probability. In particular, I work on generating structures in a quasi-entropic manner, that is, with a consumption in random bits close to the theoretical minimum amout, given by the Shannon's information limit.

Research Interests

  • Random generation of combinatorial structures
  • Symbolic combinatorics and generating functions
  • Probabilistic combinatorics
